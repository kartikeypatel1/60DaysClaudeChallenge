HEAD OF OPERATIONS · SHIFT BRIEFING

You've just taken the console. For the next 3 minutes, disruptions will hit your network in real time: ports, suppliers, trucks, warehouses, and more. Each one costs you time and money the longer it sits unresolved.

Choose the right response fast. Strong calls protect service and revenue. Weak calls — or ignoring things — quietly wreck your KPIs, sometimes with a delay.

  • Multiple alerts can be active at once — triage matters.
  • Every alert has a countdown. Let it expire and it resolves itself, badly.
  • Your goal: maximize Score while keeping KPIs healthy.

❓ Console Guide

Objective: Resolve incoming operational alerts before time runs out while protecting your KPIs and Score.

  • Priority — Critical (red, pulsing) demands immediate attention, High (orange) is urgent, Medium (cyan) can wait slightly longer.
  • Countdown — each alert auto-resolves badly if its timer hits zero.
  • Actions — every alert offers tailored responses. The best response gives the strongest KPI boost; weaker ones give partial credit; Ignore and Delay Decision carry real risk, including delayed consequences a few seconds later.
  • Difficulty — alerts arrive faster and stack up more as the 3-minute shift goes on.
