# 🚚 AI Supply Chain Control Tower

An interactive enterprise dashboard that simulates how modern organizations monitor and respond to real-time supply chain disruptions. This project provides a hands-on experience of managing logistics, inventory, suppliers, transportation, and operational KPIs through an AI-assisted Control Tower.

---

## 📖 Overview

Supply Chain Control Towers provide end-to-end visibility across enterprise operations. This project recreates that experience through an interactive dashboard where users analyze live disruptions, prioritize critical alerts, and make operational decisions while balancing business objectives.

The simulator demonstrates how supply chain leaders optimize performance by monitoring inventory, supplier reliability, transportation efficiency, customer satisfaction, and financial KPIs in real time.
