# 📦 Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

> **Day 29/60 of the Claude Challenge**

An interactive React-based simulation where players take on the role of a supply chain leader navigating enterprise-wide disruptions, balancing operational efficiency, supplier relationships, customer satisfaction, and business continuity.

---

# 📖 Overview

**Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab** is an enterprise simulation that places users in the middle of a supply chain crisis. Players must analyze business conditions, respond to unexpected disruptions, negotiate with suppliers, answer executive leadership challenges, and invest in AI technologies to strengthen operational resilience.

Designed as an educational simulation, the project transforms complex supply chain concepts into an engaging, decision-driven experience suitable for beginners and professionals alike.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Understand enterprise supply chain operations.
* Experience real-world business disruption scenarios.
* Practice strategic decision-making under pressure.
* Balance cost, inventory, delivery speed, and customer satisfaction.
* Explore how AI improves modern supply chain management.

# 🎮 Simulation Workflow

### 1️⃣ Welcome Screen

* Introduction to the simulation
* Start the crisis journey

### 2️⃣ Company Generator

Randomly generates:

* Industry
* Revenue
* Factories
* Warehouses
* Suppliers
* Countries
* Inventory Days
* Lead Time

### 3️⃣ Crisis Generator

Possible crises include:

* Factory Fire
* Supplier Bankruptcy
* Port Strike
* Cyberattack
* Flood
* Raw Material Shortage
* Political Conflict
* Shipping Delay

Each crisis includes:

* Urgency level
* Business impact
* Operational challenges

### 4️⃣ War Room

Players choose strategic actions to respond.

Business metrics dynamically change:

* Cost
* Inventory
* Profit
* Delivery Speed
* Customer Satisfaction

### 5️⃣ Supplier Negotiation

Four rounds of negotiations where every decision affects:

* Trust
* Pricing
* Lead Time

### 6️⃣ CEO Boardroom

Executive-level multiple-choice questions evaluate leadership skills and strategic thinking.

### 7️⃣ AI Strategy

Choose two AI investments from:

* Demand Forecasting
* Inventory Optimization
* Supplier Risk Monitoring
* Warehouse Vision
* Procurement Copilot

View expected business improvements after implementation.

### 8️⃣ Final Executive Dashboard

Comprehensive performance report including:

* Overall Crisis Score
* Leadership Score
* Negotiation Score
* Resilience Index
* Cost Control
* Risk Management
* Customer Satisfaction
* Personalized feedback
* Best decision
* Biggest mistake
* Expert recommendation
* Lessons learned
