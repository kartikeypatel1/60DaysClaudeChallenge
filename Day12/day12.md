## 💡 Key Learnings

### 1. A Resume Is Only the Starting Point

Most candidates focus solely on creating a resume, but recruiters evaluate much more than technical skills. Personal branding, networking, communication, and positioning play a major role in landing opportunities.

### 2. Recruiters Look for Impact

Projects should highlight:

* Problems solved
* Technologies used
* Business value created
* Learning outcomes

The focus should be on demonstrating real-world application rather than simply listing technologies.

### 3. Networking Creates Opportunities

A well-written outreach message, referral request, or LinkedIn connection request can significantly increase visibility and open doors to new opportunities.
