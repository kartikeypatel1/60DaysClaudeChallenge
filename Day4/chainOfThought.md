## What I Learned Today

Today I learned about **Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting**, a prompting technique that encourages AI models to break down complex problems into logical reasoning steps before providing the final answer.

Instead of asking AI for a direct answer, we guide it to think through the problem step-by-step. This often improves the quality, accuracy, and reasoning ability of the response.

---

## What is Chain-of-Thought Prompting?

Chain-of-Thought Prompting is a technique where we ask the AI to explain its reasoning process while solving a problem.

### Traditional Prompt

```text
How can I become a Full Stack Developer?
```

### Chain-of-Thought Prompt

```text
Act as a senior software engineer.

Analyze my current skills, identify the missing skills, create a learning roadmap, recommend projects, and explain why each step is important.
```

The second prompt produces a more structured and actionable response because it guides the AI through a reasoning process.
