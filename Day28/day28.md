# 🏥 Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator

> **Day 28/60 of the Claude Challenge**

An interactive browser-based simulation that teaches the hospital admission process through realistic healthcare workflows and decision-making scenarios.

---

## 📌 Overview

The **Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator** is an educational web application designed to help users understand how patients are prepared for hospital admission.

Instead of reading lengthy documentation, users interact with different stages of the admission process, learning how healthcare professionals coordinate documentation, insurance approvals, utilization review, and risk management before a patient is admitted.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand hospital admission workflows.
* Learn how insurance verification impacts admissions.
* Explore documentation requirements.
* Experience utilization review processes.
* Identify administrative and clinical risks.
* Improve healthcare operations knowledge through interactive learning.
