Financial Literacy & Tips

Short, practical guidance for students managing their first real income.

Pay yourself first

Move a fixed amount to savings the day your stipend lands, before it has a chance to disappear into daily spending.

Track before you cut

A week of honest tracking usually reveals more waste than any budgeting app ever will.

Small loans, big discipline

Even a modest education loan compounds. Extra payments early save more interest than the same amount later.

Emergency fund first

Before investing, build 3 months of essential expenses in an accessible account — it's your shock absorber.

Subscriptions creep

Review recurring subscriptions monthly. ₹150/month unused feels small until it's ₹1,800/year.

Start investing small

Even a ₹500/month SIP builds the habit and the math of compounding long before the amount matters.
