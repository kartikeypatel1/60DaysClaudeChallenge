# Day 18 - AI Meeting Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview

Today I built an AI-powered Meeting Intelligence Dashboard that transforms long meeting transcripts into structured business insights and actionable plans.

## Problem

Business meetings often generate large amounts of discussion, making it difficult to track:

* Key decisions
* Action items
* Risks
* Open questions
* Ownership
* Follow-ups

## Solution

Created an interactive dashboard that automatically extracts and organizes information from meeting transcripts into a clear project-management format.

## Features Implemented

### Executive Summary

* Generates concise meeting overview

### Key Takeaways

* Extracts critical discussion points
* Highlights business metrics and outcomes

### Action Item Tracker

* Task
* Owner
* Deadline
* Status

### Open Questions

* Identifies unresolved decisions
* Tracks pending approvals

### Risks & Blockers

* Detects dependencies
* Flags business risks

### Conflict Analysis

* Finds competing priorities
* Highlights scheduling and resource conflicts

### Speaker Analysis

* Speaker Summary
* Decisions by Speaker
* Action Items by Speaker
* Attribution Notes

## Insights Extracted

* Revenue Growth: 12%
* Revenue Target: 18%
* Enterprise Deals Closed: 9 of 14
* Website Traffic Growth: 34%
* Customer Retention: 94%

## Key Learnings

* AI can function as a business analyst.
* Large transcripts can be converted into structured dashboards.
* Actionable insights are more valuable than raw meeting notes.
* Clear ownership improves execution.

## Deliverables

* Interactive HTML Dashboard
* Meeting Summary
* Action Plan
* Risk Assessment
* Leadership Review Report

## Outcome

Successfully transformed an unstructured leadership meeting transcript into a professional project dashboard inspired by tools such as Notion, ClickUp, Asana, Airtable, and Linear.
