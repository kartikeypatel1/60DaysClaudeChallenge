# Day 8/60 - Claude AI Challenge

# 🌍 Personal Environmental Health Analyzer

## Overview

Today, I built a **Personal Environmental Health Analyzer** using Claude AI.

The goal of this project was to create an intelligent dashboard that analyzes environmental conditions and transforms raw air and water quality data into meaningful health insights.

Instead of simply displaying AQI numbers, the system evaluates how environmental factors can affect a person's daily life, health, and well-being.

---

## Roles Used

To design this solution, I approached the problem from multiple perspectives:

* 📊 Senior Data Analyst
* 🌱 Environmental Researcher
* 🎨 UX Designer
* 💻 Frontend Dashboard Developer

---

## Problem Statement

Environmental data is often difficult for the average person to understand.

Most AQI dashboards provide only numerical values without explaining:

* Health impacts
* Daily risks
* Water quality concerns
* Hair and skin effects
* Personalized recommendations

This project aims to bridge that gap by converting environmental metrics into actionable insights.

Today's project taught me that data becomes truly valuable when it is transformed into information people can understand and act upon.

Environmental data is not just numbers.

The challenge was not collecting data.

The challenge was presenting it in a meaningful and user-friendly way.
