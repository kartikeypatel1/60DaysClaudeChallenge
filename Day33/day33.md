# 📰 Media Integrity Analyzer

An interactive educational web application that helps users evaluate news articles and digital content before believing or sharing them. The application teaches media literacy by guiding users through headline analysis, emotional language detection, bias recognition, and critical thinking exercises.

---

## 📖 Overview

In today's digital age, misinformation spreads faster than ever. Developing media literacy is essential for identifying misleading headlines, recognizing emotional manipulation, and evaluating information objectively.

The **Media Integrity Analyzer** provides an engaging, interactive learning experience that encourages users to think critically before accepting online information as fact.

## 📚 Application Modules

### 📰 Headline Analyzer

Evaluate headlines based on:

- Clickbait indicators
- Sensational language
- Missing context
- Accuracy
- Emotional wording

---

### 🎭 Emotional Language Detector

Identify language that attempts to trigger emotions such as:

- Fear
- Anger
- Excitement
- Urgency
- Curiosity

---

### ⚖️ Bias Analyzer

Explore different forms of media bias, including:

- Political bias
- Selection bias
- Framing bias
- Confirmation bias
- Language bias

---

### 🔍 Credibility Checker

Assess content using factors like:

- Source reliability
- Supporting evidence
- Citation quality
- Transparency
- Fact consistency

---

### 🧠 Critical Thinking Challenge

Practice evaluating information by asking questions such as:

- Who created this content?
- What evidence supports the claims?
- Is there another perspective?
- What facts are missing?
- What emotions is this trying to evoke?
