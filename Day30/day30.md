# 📦 Supply Chain Optimizer

## Overview

Supply Chain Optimizer is an interactive web application that simulates real-world supply chain optimization. Instead of building a supply chain from scratch, users improve an existing system by making strategic business decisions involving suppliers, inventory, transportation, warehousing, and sustainability.

The application demonstrates how operational decisions influence key business metrics such as cost, delivery speed, customer satisfaction, and environmental impact.

## Simulation Workflow

1. Analyze the current supply chain performance.
2. Review business KPIs.
3. Identify operational bottlenecks.
4. Apply optimization strategies.
5. Compare performance before and after optimization.
6. Improve overall supply chain efficiency.
